About Us

Supplying automotive parts as combination switches, ignition switches, power window switches and electrical fuel pumps.

Shree Automobiles group was set up in 1990 with strong in-house R&D capability, is a major reason why we are competitive and successful.

We have solid personnel, know-how, production and management skills, we accept ODM orders and commit to deliver high quality and innovative products.

Nearly 300 advanced production and inspection instruments factories are included in our group which enables us to engage in precision processing, tooling development, QC etc. by sticking to APQP (Advanced Product Quality Planning).

We offer wide range of auto parts under the group of brand and keep good OEM partnerships with our clients in the global aftermarket. Our quality parts and services are highly regarded among buyers all over India.

Talk to us about world-class auto parts and OEM projects.
